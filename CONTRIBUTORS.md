## ⚖️ Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

All contributors must accept our Contributor License Agreement before code or data can be merged.

**Why?** > **«Свобода для людей, деньги для корпораций».**

Axiom uses a strict dual-licensing model:
- **Free for Humanity:** AGPL-3.0 for code, CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 for data/weights.
- **Paid for Business:** Proprietary licenses for enterprise integration.

The CLA ensures we can maintain this business model while keeping the core open for the community. By submitting a PR, you agree to the terms outlined in our `CONTRIBUTING.md`.

**Learn more:**
- [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) - Full CLA terms and contribution guidelines.
- [LICENSE](LICENSE) - The AGPLv3 Open Source License.
